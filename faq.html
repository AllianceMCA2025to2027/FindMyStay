Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about FindMyStay

What is FindMyStay?
FindMyStay is a smart platform that helps users discover, view, and book their perfect stay — complete with 360° room views, verified listings, and AI chatbot assistance.
How is FindMyStay different from other platforms?
We offer an immersive 360° virtual tour and an AI-powered chatbot to answer all your stay-related queries instantly — features most other sites lack.
Are all the listed properties verified?
Yes, every property listed on FindMyStay is verified for safety, cleanliness, and authenticity before appearing on our site.
Can I contact property owners directly?
Yes, you can connect with property owners through our secure messaging or contact option once you’re signed in.
Is there a fee to use FindMyStay?
Browsing and exploring listings is completely free! However, a small service charge may apply during confirmed bookings.
How can I list my property?
Simply click on the “List Your Property” option on our homepage and fill in your property details. Our team will verify and publish it after review.
